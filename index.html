Crystal Quest

The crystal trolls will choose a number at random at the start of the game.

You will be presented with 4 crystals that each represent an unseen amount of points.

Clicking each crystal will reveal their worth, but with each click points will be added to your total score.

Match your total score to the random number to defeat the trolls. If your total score exceeds the random number you lose.

At the start of each game, the crystals will be assigned a new amount of points.
